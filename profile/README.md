# K2X-Lab

Hi there,

Welcome to K2X-Lab.

We are a small group based in Pune, India, just getting started. Our hope is to create simple, useful tools and experiments in technology and open source. We believe in learning together, sharing what works, and building things that might help others.

Right now we are at the very beginning with no repositories yet, but we are excited about what is ahead. We plan to work on AI tools, developer utilities, and whatever feels meaningful as we go.

If you are curious or want to follow along, you are welcome here. When we have something ready, we will share it openly and hope to hear your thoughts.

We are looking forward to this journey.

Made with hope in Pune, Maharashtra.
